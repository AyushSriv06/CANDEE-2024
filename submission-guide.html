Instructions for Participants:

  1. Download the extended abstract Template for the participants, and prepare a 1-page extended abstract. Please, make sure to include at least one figure and a brief summary of your findings in the extended abstract to enhance the chances of acceptance of your abstract for the conference.
  2. Download the Template for POSTER Presentation.
  3. Register using your E-mail. The REGISTER tab is available in the Right most corner of Navigation (Header) Bar.
  4. Login and complete your user profile. Now, you will be able to access your User DASHBOARD.
  5. Click on Submit/View Abstracts in your DASHBOARD to submit your extended abstract. (The participants may send the abstracts through email. However, prefer submission through your DASHBOARD.)
