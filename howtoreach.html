Getting Around the City

Within the city, tempos and auto rickshaws are the main modes of conveyance. These tempos and auto rickshaws could be hired completely or ridden on shared basis. They ply throughout the city and even to adjoining areas.

ABV-IIITM campus is about 7 km from Gwalior railway station and about 12 km from city airport. Auto rickshaws and tempos are available from railway station to institute campus with maximum charge of Rs. 100/-. From airport you could hire cabs or auto-rickshaws with a maximum charge of Rs. 200/-. The institute has its own bus service for carrying passengers to and from the institute to important locations of the city.
