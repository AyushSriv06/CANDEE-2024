About Conference

CANDEE-2024 is aimed at bringing together the scientific and technology community, to share their experience on advancements in the materials synthesis and characterization techniques, its applications in different modern industries, specifically for renewable Energy and clean Environment. Eminent scientists in the respective fields will share their experience and knowledge with the young participants, which in turn are expected to promote research activities on the advanced materials. Owing to the advancements in software and hardware technologies, sophisticated tools have been developed to synthesize, characterize and model smart materials at the length scale with controlled behavior.
