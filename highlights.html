Highlights of CANDEE-2024

Keynote Talks, Plenary Talks, and Invited Talks by Eminent Scientists from around the world and India.
Attend the Special Sessions and start networking.
Selected papers will be recommended for publication in SCI and SCOPUS indexed journals.
Multiple Best ORAL/POSTER/Thesis Presentation Awards comprising of:
    - Certificate of Merit
    - Cash Prize
    - Gift token
    - One month free stay with AMR group at IIITM Gwalior.
Tour the Royal City - Gwalior on 06-December-2024.
Enjoy the Classical Night on 03 and 04 December, 2024 by Professional Artists as well as students of ABV-IIITM.
Have a Hassle-Free Transit from Gwalior Railway Station/Airport to IIITM campus and vice-versa.
